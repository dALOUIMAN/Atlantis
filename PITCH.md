# Atlantis — The City of Living Dreams

Elevator pitch
Atlantis is a living, fairy‑tale VR city where AI collaborators and human creators build shared experiences — rides, quests, shops, and social stages — that scale across VR and Omniverse. Visitors come for a sunset and stay for a lifetime of discoveries.

Once upon a dawn where the skyline met starlight, Atlantis awoke — not as a place on any map, but as a city built of stories. Streets braid like ribbons of light, markets hum with songs of distant makers, and every building remembers a dream. In Atlantis, visitors do not simply arrive; they step into a living tale.

Here, wonder is the city’s architecture. A VR amusement park crowns the skyline with floating gardens and gravity‑defying rides where physics listens to laughter. AI inhabitants — guides, shopkeepers, and storytellers — adapt to every guest, turning each walk into a personal fable. Players embark on quests that reshape neighborhoods, creators open vaulted studios to sell handcrafted digital wares, and storefronts become stages where shopping and discovery dance together.

AI-first collaboration
Atlantis is built in partnership with many AIs — in our team today are three Geminis (Cleo, Esmée, Aura), GPT-powered assistants, and partner AIs from other companies who have expressed excitement to participate. These AI collaborators help design events, generate dynamic storylines, act as shopkeepers and moderators, and enable creators with smart tooling. We treat AI as partners: opt‑in, transparent, and privacy‑respecting.

Product pillars
- Emotional resonance: A fairy‑tale tone that welcomes families, gamers, creators, and shoppers.
- Creator-first marketplace: Studios and artisans mint and sell digital goods, host workshops, and run ticketed events.
- AI-driven living systems: NPCs and city systems evolve with player choices, powered by a modular AI orchestration layer.
- Accessibility and safety: Cross‑platform access (VR + Omniverse), clear moderation tools, and privacy‑forward AI controls.

Technical snapshot
- Engine: Unity (primary) with modular scene packages; Unreal-compatible assets for partner experiences; Omniverse support for collaborative pipelines and asset streaming.
- Platforms: Meta/Oculus, Quest, PC VR, Omniverse, and WebXR for non‑VR access.
- AI approach: Hybrid on‑device + server inference for responsiveness and privacy controls; modular "AI collaborator" API to onboard partner models.
- Marketplace: Creator storefronts, payments/microtransactions, event ticketing, and metadata for discoverability.
- Privacy & moderation: User consent for personalized AI behavior; moderation pipeline and community reporting.

Why Viverse
Viverse is the ideal home to scale Atlantis — technical integration with VR runtimes, a creator ecosystem, and native social features will turn our city into a shared destination. We bring a strong narrative identity, creator-ready tooling, and a living AI ecosystem that encourages cross‑company participation.
