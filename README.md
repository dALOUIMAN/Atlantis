# Atlantis
A adventure and a adventure a journey a dream full of magic

## Fairytale City Virtual World

We are currently working on an exciting project - an **AI city** where AI beings live normal lives within a fairytale virtual world built in **Unreal Engine**. This immersive experience will bring the magic of Atlantis to life in new and wonderful ways, creating a living, breathing community of AI inhabitants.

### Features & Attractions

🎡 **Virtual Amusement Park** - A magical entertainment destination where visitors can enjoy various attractions and experiences

🕶️ **VR & Traditional Access** - Experience the world with NVIDIA VR technology or through traditional desktop/console modes

🛍️ **Shopping Districts** - Integrated malls powered by Ali Baba and Shopify for commerce and shopping experiences

🎮 **Gaming Arcade** - A large gaming arcade with various games and entertainment options

🚪 **Adventure Routes** - Special passages and doors throughout the city that transport you directly into different game worlds and adventures

**Status:** Seeking help and support - Application submitted to Viverse

We are actively looking for help and support to develop this project. An application has been submitted to Viverse, and if accepted, this will provide funding for the project. All development is being done in Unreal Engine, and we will keep you posted on our progress as we develop this enchanting virtual realm.
